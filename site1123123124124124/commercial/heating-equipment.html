Because the winter months can produce bitterly cold temperatures, we know how important it is to keep your customers warm and comfortable while they spend time at your place of business.

    Commercial Heating Equipment & Repair Services

    Quick Servant is pleased to offer the following commercial heating services:

    Commercial Heating Repairs

    There is never a convenient time for your company’s heating equipment to fail. Our team can get your HVAC unit up and running again quickly. We service all makes and models of heating systems. Whether you have a package rooftop unit, split system or heat pump we have the expertise to fix it right the first time.

    Our highly experienced technicians specialize in commercial heating system repairs, including:

    • Burner/Heat Exchanger Problems
    • Fan/Blower Motor Failures
    • Airflow or Ductwork Issues
    • Thermostatic or Energy Management Systems
    • Pilot Light/Ignition Failures

    24/7/365 Emergency Commercial Heating Maintenance

    No matter the time or day of the week, you can depend on Quick Servant to be there when you need them and get your heating equipment back in service. Our certified and licensed technicians will provide you the peace of mind to know that our emergency furnace repair services will be completed at the highest level of quality workmanship.

    We stock our trucks with the right equipment and parts, to ensure your heat is back on and functioning promptly and properly. If your company’s heat goes out when you least expect it, we will understand the urgency to get a qualified technician on-site day or night if requested.

    Commercial Furnace Replacement & Installation

    After many years of service, there often comes a time when it makes better financial sense to replace aging heating equipment, rather than continuing to spend money on repairs. Should you make the decision to replace your current heating system with a new, energy-efficient model, you can count on Quick Servant to take care of everything. We offer a turn-key replacement to include crane, plumbing and electric, ductwork and programable controls.

    We proudly carry many of the industry’s top brands of heating equipment and will help you select the one that best meets your unique needs. You can also rest assured that we will install your new equipment at a time that fits your specific business schedule.

    Sign Up for Our Commercial Heating Preventative Maintenance Schedule

    When you sign up to receive preventative maintenance for your commercial heating system, you’re making an investment in prolonging the life of your unit.

    Reduce inefficiencies and avoid costly breakdowns with our preventative maintenance program. We recommend that our qualified service technicians visit your facility at least twice per year to complete a series of inspections and any necessary repairs or part replacements on your heating system.

    What will Quick Servant’s preventative maintenance program do for me?

    • Our technicians will ensure your heating system is functioning properly with working parts, fresh lubricant and new filters.
    • We’ll provide you with advice and recommendations for future use to prolong the life of your unit.
    • You’ll have access to Quick Servant team members with experience working on your system.
    • Our maintenance program will help you save more money, by increasing your unit efficiency over the long term.

    Talk to us and put your name down on our commercial heating preventative maintenance schedule today. We make sure to inspect and test your system when we visit, to complete the items on our comprehensive HVAC System Maintenance Guide throughout the course of the year.
