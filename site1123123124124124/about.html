    • Welcome to Quick Servant!

    When inviting a contractor to perform a service in your business, you look for someone with years of experience and a level of integrity to match. You want a company that understands your individual needs and will provide the quick and dependable services to satisfy them.

    When lifelong Maryland resident John W. Peters established Quick Servant in 1979, this is exactly the kind of company he had in mind. Today, the Quick Servant team continues to carry out John’s vision by providing top quality HVAC, refrigeration, cooking equipment, Restaurant Repair and building maintenance services to commercial businesses every day.

    When you come to us for service, we aim to become the only contractor you’ll ever need to call. We build long-term partnerships with our customers by providing prompt, reliable service and always standing behind our work. Our job isn’t to simply meet your expectations – it’s to exceed them.

    We’re Proud of Our Services

    Quick Servant specializes in all aspects of heating, cooling and ventilation services, as well as commercial refrigeration, cooking equipment and building maintenance. Our experienced technicians can provide all of the services listed below, and more!

    Commercial:

    • HVAC repair, replacement and maintenance
    • Refrigeration repair, replacement and maintenance
    • Cooking equipment repair, replacement and maintenance
    • Building maintenance and repair, including minor electric and plumbing services

     

    We’ll Be There When You Need Us™

    At Quick Servant, we make it our goal to efficiently solve your problem and make ourselves readily available should an issue arise. That’s why we keep two supervisors and 10 technicians on call at all times. Whether your AC breaks down in the middle of summer or your restaurant’s refrigeration system gives out just before the dinner rush, our technicians are here to help 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

    When you call Quick Servant for an emergency service, you’ll always receive a real live person on the line – no machines. And you’ll never need to call more than once for prompt service! For each and every service call our technicians respond to, be it in the middle of the night or on a holiday, we don’t leave until the problem is fixed and the customer is happy.

    Solving the Problems Others Can’t

    When you’re facing a complex mechanical issue in your business, you want to hire a company you can trust to solve the problem and get it right. At Quick Servant, we pride ourselves on being able to solve the problems that other companies can’t. Our technicians receive the highest level of factory training within their respective fields and they are constantly learning about new techniques and tools to help them tackle new challenges with innovative solutions.
